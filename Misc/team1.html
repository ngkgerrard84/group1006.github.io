Ethical issues relating to mobile phone applications

In my opinion, having such access to the vast information available on the internet could be a double-edged sword as it is great to use for educational purposes and also is possible for adolescents to expand their horizons. However, the internet is also dangerous, this is because data leaks could cause young people's life to be endangered as they might not have fully grasped the danger of putting their personal information up online for everyone to see.
