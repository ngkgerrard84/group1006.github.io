Technology Risks

Mobile apps can cause mental stress

According to providence, there have been several studies looking into the relationship between devices and young people. In the article, the author stated that a research conducted by the University of Arizona showed that teenagers who are addicted to tehir smart phone are more likely to face depression or lonliness

Privacy issues

Recently, about 3.5 billion people's personal data was compromised and stolen, this shows the risks of using application as there is always a threat of your personal information getting compromised

Anxiety and lowering self-esteem

Many parents thinks that exposure to technology might affect a teenager's development. According to the Child Mind Insititute. A survery conducted by the Royal Society for Public Health saw results saying Snapchat, Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram all lead to increased feelings of depression, anxiety, and poor body image. All of these are free mobile apps that anyone could access on their phone at anytime.
